Chris Ross

 

INTERESTS -

Sci-Fi junky, futurist, tinkerer, storyteller, dreamer and enthusiastic creator – I’m always looking for something that hasn’t been tried. I’m seeking a path toward new and exciting tech that will allow me to make my mark on the world. Human augmentation is my passion. Optimism is my shield. Resolve is my weapon. I blaze new trails for future civilizations. I will boldly code where no man has code before.

HISTORY -

My history has been filled with lots of bad choices, failures, and regrets. But there have been some shining moments as well. When I was born Death flipped a coin; Heads - you receive your mother's gene and live a full life, Tails - you receive your father's gene and ALS withers you away to nothing when you reach your 30s. I lived as if I had nothing to lose, in the moment, never planning ahead. Then one day the hand that covered the coin was lifted - Heads... I had won the round, but much of my family wouldn't be as lucky. I need to make it mean something.

EDUCATION -

I received an Associates in Drafting and Design at ITT Tech while learning all about civil, structural, and mechanical schematics. While I was there I got a lot of experience using the Autodesk suite of programs including Revit, Inventor, and AutoCAD. Since then my interests have shifted and I’m currently working toward a bachelor’s degree in Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile Technologies at Boise State University’s College of Innovation and Design. Our field of study pushes deep into interactive programming and storytelling, game and audio-visual design, and web and mobile development.
